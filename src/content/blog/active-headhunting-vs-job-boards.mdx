True talent acquisition requires a shift from passive processing to aggressive market penetration.

- **Passive sourcing:** Posting a job ad and filtering through hundreds of mismatched profiles.
- **Active headhunting:** Directly identifying top-performing engineers at competitor firms and mapping out custom talent networks.
